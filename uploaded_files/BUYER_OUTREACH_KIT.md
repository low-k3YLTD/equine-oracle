1. **Immediately**: Reply with calendar link (Calendly, free tier)
2. **24 hours before**: Send one-pager and demo video
3. **After call**: Send thank you + next steps within 2 hours

### If They Say No

```
Thanks for the quick response, [Name]. Completely understand if this 
isn't the right fit or timing.

If anything changes on your end—or if you know someone in the industry 
who might be interested—I'd appreciate the introduction.

Best of luck with [Company]'s initiatives.

[Your name]
```

## Key Talking Points for Calls

### Opening (30 seconds)
"Equine Oracle is a machine learning prediction platform for horse racing. 
We've achieved 95.3% accuracy on top-finisher predictions—20 percentage 
points above industry baseline. The technology is production-ready and 
we're exploring strategic acquisition."

### The Problem (30 seconds)
"Horse racing prediction is stuck in the dark ages—most tipsters use 
intuition, not data. Serious bettors and bookmakers need an edge, but 
existing ML solutions are either inaccurate or not production-ready."

### Your Solution (60 seconds)
"We've built a weighted ensemble of 5+ specialized models that:
1. Achieves 95%+ accuracy on race outcomes
2. Delivers predictions in under 150ms
3. Retrains automatically as new data comes in
4. Integrates via API for any platform

The IP is proprietary—the ensemble weighting algorithm and feature 
engineering are ours alone."

### Traction (30 seconds)
"We've validated with [X] beta users, achieved [metric], and the system 
has processed [X] predictions with [accuracy]% accuracy."

### The Ask (30 seconds)
"We're looking for a strategic partner who can scale this technology. 
We're open to acquisition, exclusive licensing, or partnership. What 
makes sense for [Company]?"

## Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We're not looking to acquire" | "Understood. Would you be open to an exclusive licensing arrangement instead?" |
| "We build in-house" | "Fair. How long would it take to achieve 95% accuracy? We've spent [X] months getting here." |
| "The market is too small" | "NZ/AU racing is NZD 500M+. Globally it's USD 8B+. And this tech applies to other sports." |
| "We need to see more traction" | "What metrics would convince you? I'm happy to focus on specific validation." |
| "The price is too high" | "What's your budget range? There may be a structure that works for both of us." |

## Closing Tactics

### Create Urgency (Genuine)
"We're in active discussions with [X] parties. I'd love for [Company] to 
have a shot, but we need to move in the next 30 days."

### Make It Easy
"I can have our lawyer draft a simple LOI this week. If the numbers work, 
we can move to due diligence immediately."

### Ask for the Close
"Does this feel like a fit? If so, what are the next steps on your end?"
